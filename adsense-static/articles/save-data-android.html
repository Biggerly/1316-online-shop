7 Ways to Save Mobile Data on Android

  • Turn on Data Saver in Settings.
  • Restrict background data for heavy apps.
  • Use Lite versions of apps where possible.
  • Lower video quality in streaming apps.
  • Disable auto‑play in social media apps.
  • Download over Wi‑Fi only.
  • Use a browser with data compression.
