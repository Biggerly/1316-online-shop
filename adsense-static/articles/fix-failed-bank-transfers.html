What to Do If a Bank Transfer Fails

  1. Wait 10–30 minutes — many reversals are automatic.
  2. Check your bank app for pending transactions.
  3. Keep the transaction reference ID handy.
  4. Contact your bank via in‑app chat or nearest branch.
  5. Escalate with a formal complaint if beyond 24 hours.
  6. For merchant issues, contact the merchant and your bank.

Always verify recipient details before retrying the transfer.
