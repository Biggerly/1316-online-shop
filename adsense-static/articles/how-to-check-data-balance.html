How to Check Data Balance on All Networks

Here are the quickest ways to check your data balance on MTN, Airtel, Glo, and 9mobile.

MTN

  • USSD: Dial *323# and follow the prompt for data balance.
  • SMS: Send 2 to 323.
  • App: Open myMTN NG and check the dashboard.

Airtel

  • USSD: Dial *323# or *140#.
  • SMS: Send STATUS to 323.
  • App: Open Airtel Thanks and check the home screen.

Glo

  • USSD: Dial *323# or *127*0#.
  • SMS: Send INFO to 127.
  • App: Open Glo Cafe for balances.

9mobile

  • USSD: Dial *323# or *200#.
  • SMS: Send INFO to 323.
  • App: Open my9mobile and view remaining data.

Tips

  • If a code fails, try again later or use the network app.
  • Ensure your SIM has network service (bars) before dialing USSD.
